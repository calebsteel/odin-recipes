NoMeatloaf

Instructions

Filling

  • 2 lbs Beyond Ground Beef
  • 1/2 onion, minced
  • 1 cup unsweetened non dairy milk
  • 1 cup plain instant oats
  • 1/4 cup Justt Egg
  • salt and pepper to taste

Topping

  • 2/3 cup ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 table yellow mustard

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees
  2. Combine all Ingredients for the filling into a large mixing bowl and combine thouroughly.
  3. Grease a 9x5 loaf pan and evenly spread filling in the bottom.
  4. Combine all Ingredients for the topping into a bowl and mix until smooth.
  5. Spread topping evenly over the filling.
