Vegan Cream of Chicken

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons vegan butter
  • 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup mild-flavored plant milk
  • 1/2 cup vegan chicken style broth
  • sea salt to taste
  • ground black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Set a small saucepan over medium heat, and add the butter
  2. When butter is melted, add flour; whisk until smooth
  3. Add milk, broth, salt, and pepper
  4. Bring to a boil and continue whisking for 2 minutes, until thickened.
  5. Use anywhere a recipe calls for acan of cream of chicken soup!
